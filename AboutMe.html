  • About Me

    • Hello, my name is Ericka Allen. I am a first-year librarian at W.R. Castle Memorial Elementary.
    I have had an exceptional year helping kids develop their love of reading in our library.

    While helping inspire kids to read daily, I have also been inspired by their imagination and creativity.
    I have been designing PBL units to help my students connect with their reading while considering real-life experiences.

    To help encourage my students to read, my school uses the Accelerated Reader program. I coordinate with my FRYSC director to help purchase AR incentives.
