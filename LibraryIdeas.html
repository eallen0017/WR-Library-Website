Interesting Bookshelves

  • Rent a bookshelf: Students rent and decorate a bookshelf, going along with a theme or genre, displaying books and adding personal style.
  • Mystery Book: Display and number books displayed on a bookshelf, have students draw a number at random and check out the coordinating book. A fun way to help a stuck student find a new book. If they don't like it, they can always repeat and try again.

Jokes For Library Display Seasonally Ordered

  1. Spring: Why is the Grinch such a good gardener? He has a green thumb.
  2. Summer: How do you catch a smart fish? With a bookworm.
  3. Autumn: Why did Dracula go to the library? He wanted to sink his teeth into a good book.
  4. Winter: How do libraries make sure novels stay warm? They give them book jackets.
