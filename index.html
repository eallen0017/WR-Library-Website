Hello, my name is Ericka Allen I am a first year librarian at W.R. Castle Memorial Elementary.
I have had an exceptional year helping kids develop their love of reading in our library.

While helping inspire kids to read daily, I have also been inspired by thier imagination and creativity.
I have been designing PBL units to help my students connect to their reading while considering real life experiences.

To help encourage my studnets to read, my school uses the Accelerated Reader program. I coordinate with my FRYSC director to help purchase AR incentives.
